Customization

Customibility for anything.

Our plan with this revolutionary mouse is to make every part able to be removable and replacable. From the individual wires and lights to the switches and sensors all will be able to removed and replaced.

Percision

Precise as you are.

By putting the mouse sensor on the tip of your finger, you allow the flexability and percicion that your finger has to bring your gaming experience to the next level. We recommend using an aim trainer to train your finger to learn the movements it needs to make.

Custom Built

Built for the consumer.

All of our glove mice are custom built to fit each of our customers needs. We will custom build every mouse we build to fit the wants of the consumer. We will build it with specific sensors at several places on the hand.
